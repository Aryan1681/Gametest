Game Logic Plan

Keep it simple now; expand during the prototype week.

Core Loop

  • Player action: tap/click blue water drops before they hit the ground.
  • Avoid: gray pollutant drops; tapping them costs 1 life.
  • Timer: 30 seconds per round.
  • Scoring: +10 per clean drop, +5 combo on streaks of 5, −5 if clean drop hits ground.
  • Lives: start with 3. Lose 1 on pollutant tap. 0 lives ends round early.

Feedback

  • Pop animation on good taps; shake on pollutant.
  • Banner shows milestones: 100, 200, 300 points with short messages about clean-water impact.
  • End screen shows score, best, and CTA to learn/donate.

Brand Use

  • Keep name on one line and use Jerry Can as primary mark.
  • Prefer logo on white or black. Don’t place over faces; keep legibility high.
  • Imagery tone: hopeful and respectful; focus on possibility, not guilt.
